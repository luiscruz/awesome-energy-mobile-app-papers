      <h1 id="awesome-mobile-app-energy-papers">Awesome Mobile App Energy Papers</h1>
<p><a href="https://github.com/sindresorhus/awesome"><img src="/media/badge.svg" alt="Awesome" /></a></p>

<p>As a result of my research as a PhD student (supervised by <a href="https://ruimaranhao.com">Prof. Rui Abreu</a>), I have listed academic literature that is related to the energy efficiency of mobile applications.</p>

<p>The goal of this list is to provide relevant academic papers to mobile developers, researchers, and students that want to extend their knowledge in this research topic.</p>

<p>If you know an academic paper that should be listed here, please contribute. As rule of thumb, the paper needs to be accepted in a relevant peer-reviewed conference/journal. Please, read the <a href="/contributing.md">contribution guidelines</a>.</p>

<p>This list is based on similar lists of papers in other topics: <a href="https://github.com/pFarb/awesome-crypto-papers">Awesome Crypto Papers</a>; <a href="https://meta.stackexchange.com/questions/134495/academic-papers-using-stack-exchange-data">Stack Exchange Academic Papers</a>. It is sorted by year. Eventually, the papers might be categorized according to their approach and the problem they address.</p>

<p>For news and related content check out my <a href="https://luiscruz.github.io/">website</a> and follow me on <a href="https://twitter.com/luismcruz">Twitter</a>.</p>
